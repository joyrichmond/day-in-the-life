A Day as Arya's Momma

Morning

  • 7:30am Arya wakes up.
  • 7:40am Momma gets up to get Arya's bottle ready, give a good morning kiss, and change her diaper.
  • 7:45am Arya has her first bottle on the porch. When she's done, we rock for a while watching the birds.
  • 8:15am Play time for Arya while Momma spends some time with Jesus.
  • 9:00am We go inside and Momma has breakfast while Arya plays in the living room.
  • 9:30am Momma gets dressed and does her makeup and hair so she can feel good about going in public.
  • 10:00am Arya has bottle number 2 and gets a diaper change before going down for nap number 1.
  • 10-12pm Momma has free time. Sometimes I get chores done, or mow the grass, or do some online shopping, or work on CoderGirl tasks.

Afternoon

  • 12:00pm Arya wakes up, eats, and gets another diaper change.
  • 12-2pm Shopping time! This is when we get to go out and run errands before Arya has to take another nap.
  • 2:00pm Arya eats, gets a diaper change, and goes down for nap number 2.
  • 4:00pm Arya wakes up, eats, gets a diaper change (sounding repetitive yet?).
  • 4-5pm Arya plays.
  • 5:00pm Arya eats actual food. Gotta try those new flavors!
  • 5:30pm Bath time!
  • 6:00pm Eat, diaper change, down for the night with a song and a kiss.

Evening

  • 6:00pm This is also about when Chris gets home.
  • 6:00-10:00pm We watch some tv, eat dinner, talk, sit on the porch, and just in general hang out.
  • 10:00pm I head to bed. Brush my teeth, wash my face. Read the next chapter in my riveting book that I read to go to sleep, but hate putting down when I do.
