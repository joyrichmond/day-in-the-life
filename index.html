A Day as Arya's Momma

Morning

  • 7:30 am
    Arya wakes up.
  • 7:40 am
    Momma gets up to get Arya's bottle ready, give a good morning kiss, and change her diaper.
  • 7:45 am
    Arya has her first bottle on the porch. When she's done, we rock for a while watching the birds.
  • 8:15 am
    Play time for Arya while Momma spends some time with Jesus.
  • 9:00 am
    We go inside and Momma has breakfast while Arya plays in the living room.
  • 9:30 am
    Momma gets dressed and does her makeup and hair so she can feel good about going in public.
  • 10:00 am
    Arya has bottle number 2 and gets a diaper change before going down for nap number 1.
  • 10-12 pm
    Momma has free time. Sometimes I get chores done, or mow the grass, or do some online shopping, or work on CoderGirl tasks.

Afternoon

  • 12:00 pm
    Arya wakes up, eats, and gets another diaper change.
  • 12-2 pm
    Shopping time! This is when we get to go out and run errands before Arya has to take another nap.
  • 2:00 pm
    Arya eats, gets a diaper change, and goes down for nap number 2.
  • 4:00 pm
    Arya wakes up, eats, gets a diaper change (sounding repetitive yet?).
  • 4-5 pm
    Arya plays.
  • 5:00 pm
    Arya eats actual food. Gotta try those new flavors!
  • 5:30 pm
    Bath time!
  • 6:00 pm
    Eat, diaper change, down for the night with a song and a kiss.

Evening

  • 6:00 pm
    This is also about when Chris gets home.
  • 6-10 pm
    We watch some tv, eat dinner, talk, sit on the porch, and just in general hang out like best-friend hubby-wives do.
  • 10:00 pm
    I head to bed. Brush my teeth, wash my face. Read the next chapter in my riveting book that I read to go to sleep, but hate putting down when I do.
  • 12:00 am
    Mom. Sleep. Repeat.
